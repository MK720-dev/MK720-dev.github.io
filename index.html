About Me

I'm a Computer Science student originally from the Mediterranean, now studying at the University of Kansas. My interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, optimization, biomedical imaging, and human–AI interaction. A knee injury sparked my fascination with biomechanics and rehabilitation, motivating my work on AI-enhanced clinical tools, optimization-aware ML frameworks, and computational modeling of biological structures.
