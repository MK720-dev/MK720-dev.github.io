About Me

I'm a Computer Science student originally from the Mediterranean, now studying at the University of Kansas. My interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, optimization, biomedical imaging, and human–AI interaction. A knee injury years ago sparked my curiosity in biomechanics — and since then I’ve been combining ML + modeling to build tools that help us better understand human movement and improve rehabilitation.
